Soundscapes In Videogames

Uhhh... What's a Soundscape?

Ok, you might not have heard of these before. To be honest, I kinda made them up, but a Soundscape is the overall Sound Profile of a game. Music, SFX, and Ambience. This page won't really go into much more detail about what a Soundscape is, but instead rate various games based on their Soundscapes.

Celeste

Celeste's Soundscape is expertly crafted, with the previously-mentioned adaptive music, as well as semi-adaptive ambience and very pleasurable SFX.

Best Sound Elements:

  • The Soundtrack
  • Crush Blocks, AKA "Kevins"
  • The Dash Sound

Overall Rating:

9.5/10

Risk Of Rain 2

Risk Of Rain 2's Soundscape is rather lacking compared to its Soundtrack. It has decent enough SFX, but the ambience is baked into the Soundtrack and therefore can have awkward transitions at the start of bossfights.

Best Sound Elements:

  • The Soundtrack
  • Teleporter Sounds
  • Enemy Sounds

Overall Rating:

7.5/10

Terraria

Terraria lacks much ambience but its SFX work incredibly well with the artsyle.

Best Sound Elements:

  • The Soundtrack
  • Enemy Sounds
  • Weapon Sounds

Overall Rating:

8/10
